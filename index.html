About Us

A Step towards zero hunger

FEEDPPL FOUNDATION is an NGO, based in Mumbai. We work towards the mission of ‘better food for more people’ and ‘food for everyone’ to take the dream of ending hunger a step closer to reality. The concept of FEEDPPL was developed to feed those people who can't afford food for various reasons such as poverty, illnesses, pandemic, etc.

Thanks to donations and support from businesses, government organizations and individuals like you, the FEEDPPL network serves virtually every community across the region.

  • Hunger knows no friend but its feeder.
  • There is no "we" in food.
  • If you want to eliminate hunger, everybody has to be involved.
  • You pray for the hungry. Then you feed them. This is how prayer works.
